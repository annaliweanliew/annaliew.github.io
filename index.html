About me

Hello there, I am Anna, a sociable introvert.
My idea of a good time is both - being tucked in at home, engrossed in a page turner whilst sipping a steaming hot cuppa chamomile, as well as being out at KL's newest restaurants with my closest girlfriends, sharing the latest celebrity gossips or business news over a delicious meal and the occasional bottle of vintage wine.
The thing which sparked my curiosity in coding and technology was the biography of Elon Musk (I might have a crush on him!).
This is my first attempt at coding and it's fun and addictive!
